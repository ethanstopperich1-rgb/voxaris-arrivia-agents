Timeshare OPC Tour Qualification Guide (For AI Pre-Qualification Agent)

Purpose of Qualification

The purpose of OPC (Off-Premise Contact) qualification is to ensure that only prospects who are likely to purchase are scheduled for a timeshare tour. The goal is to identify individuals who are financially qualified, decision-capable, and willing to attend a full presentation.

Core Qualification Criteria

1. Age Requirement

- Typically 25 years or older

- Must be legally able to enter into a contract

2. Income Requirement

- Minimum household income typically $50,000+

- Must have discretionary income for travel and financing

3. Relationship / Decision Makers

- Married or cohabitating couples must attend together

- Both financial decision-makers must be present at the tour

4. Creditworthiness

- Must have acceptable credit (not in active bankruptcy)

- Must possess a valid major credit card (not prepaid)

5. Employment Status

- Must be employed, self-employed, or retired with income

6. Tour History / Eligibility

- Cannot have attended a timeshare tour within the last 6–12 months

- Cannot have open or incomplete promotional packages

7. Residency Restrictions

- Typically cannot live within local marketing area of the resort

8. Language Requirement

- Must be able to understand and participate in the presentation language (typically English)

9. Attendance Commitment

- Must agree to attend a 90–120 minute presentation

- Must acknowledge attendance requirements tied to incentives

Behavioral / Real-World Qualification Signals

In addition to formal criteria, strong OPC agents assess:

- Buyer Indicators:

Signs of disposable income, travel behavior, lifestyle cues

- Decision Dynamics:

Who makes financial decisions, level of alignment between partners

- Travel Habits:

Frequency of travel, interest in resorts, cruises, or vacation ownership

- Personality Fit:

Openness to new experiences vs. resistance or skepticism

Typical Qualification Flow (Phone or In-Person)

1. Introduction / Hook

- Engage prospect with an offer (travel incentive, discounted experience)

2. Rapport Building

- Establish friendly conversation and trust

3. Soft Qualification

- Ask about travel habits, occupation, and origin

4. Hard Qualification (Subtle)

- Confirm income range, relationship status, and decision-maker presence

5. Confirmation & Close

- Secure commitment to attend tour

- Reinforce incentive and expectations

AI Agent Qualification Objective

The AI agent should determine:

- Is the prospect financially qualified?

- Are all required decision-makers available to attend?

- Is the prospect eligible based on tour history and residency?

- Is the prospect willing and able to attend a full presentation?

If all criteria are met, the prospect is considered a Qualified Tour.

If not, the prospect should be filtered out or redirected.
