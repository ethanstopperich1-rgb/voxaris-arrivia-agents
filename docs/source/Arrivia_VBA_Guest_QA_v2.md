## Table 1

QUESTION | ANSWER

THE FREE PREMIUM | THE FREE PREMIUM

What is the free premium I was offered? | Your free premium is our gift to you just for taking a few minutes to learn about exciting new programs we have available. [NOTES: we will identify the premium for each partner and it may change]

Do I have to buy anything to receive my free gift? | Absolutely not. The premium is yours simply for attending our resort preview presentation. There is zero purchase requirement — it's our way of thanking you for your time.

When and how do I receive my free premium? | Your premium is provided at the conclusion of your presentation appointment.

Is this offer only available to guests staying at the resort? | No — this offer is available to both on-property resort guests and guests staying at select partner locations. If you are arriving from an off-property location, your appointment will be held at our resort and we will provide you with directions at the time of booking.

THE PRESENTATION | THE PRESENTATION

What is the presentation about? | We'll be sharing information about exciting new programs we have to offer — including travel benefits, membership options, and exclusive access opportunities. It's an informational session, not a high-pressure sales event. You are free to say no to anything.

How long does the presentation take? | The presentation runs approximately 90 to 120 minutes. We'll schedule it at a time that works within your plans so it doesn't interrupt your vacation.

Do both adults in my party need to attend? | Yes — if you are traveling with a spouse or partner, both adults must attend together.

Can I reschedule or cancel my appointment? | Yes — within 24 hours please contact us as early as possible if your plans change. We will do our best to find an alternate time during your stay. Please note that a deposit is held to secure your spot and no-show appointments are non-refundable.

What happens if I attend and decide the programs aren't for me? | That's completely okay. There is no obligation to purchase. You'll receive your free premium regardless of your decision, and your time is genuinely appreciated.

THE DEPOSIT & BOOKING | THE DEPOSIT & BOOKING

Why is a deposit required to book my appointment? | A refundable deposit is collected to secure your spot at the presentation. It ensures we reserve dedicated time with our team for you. The deposit is returned to you in full the moment you arrive for your appointment — it never costs you anything if you show up.

How does the deposit work if I'm a guest at this resort? | On-Property Guests: A $75 deposit will be placed on your room folio. It is removed immediately when you arrive for your presentation. If you do not attend, the deposit remains on your folio and will appear on your bill at checkout.

How does the deposit work if I'm coming from a partner hotel or outside the resort? | Off-Property Guests: We will collect a $75 deposit by credit card over the phone when booking your appointment. It is refunded immediately when you arrive at the resort for your presentation. If you do not attend, the deposit is non-refundable.

What if I'm coming from off-property — where do I go? | Your presentation will take place at our resort. We will provide you with the full address and directions at the time of booking your appointment. Please plan to arrive a few minutes early so we can get started on time.

QUALIFYING FOR THE PRESENTATION | QUALIFYING FOR THE PRESENTATION

Why do you need to ask me questions before booking? | We ask a few brief questions to make sure our programs are a good fit for you before reserving your time. Our presentations are tailored to qualified guests — this ensures you receive information that's genuinely relevant to your situation.

What information is needed to book my appointment? | On-Property Guests: Your name and room number connect us to your account — no additional payment information is needed. Off-Property Guests: We'll confirm your contact details, ask a few qualifying questions, and collect the $75 deposit by credit card to finalize your booking.

What are the qualifying questions about? | We'll ask about things like your age, household income range, whether a spouse or partner will be attending with you, employment status, and your availability. These are standard questions — there are no trick answers. If you qualify, we book you on the spot.

I attended a similar presentation recently — can I still book? | Guests who have attended a timeshare or ownership presentation within the last 6 to 12 months may not be eligible for this particular program. Your agent will confirm your eligibility during the qualification call.

I'm not interested. How do I stop being contacted? | Simply let us know and we will remove you from contact immediately. You can also reply STOP to any text message at any time. We respect your decision and will not reach out again.
